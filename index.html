How do you keep spaces livable during interior projects?

We phase rooms, maintain walkways, protect floors and furniture, and clean daily. We can sequence around critical areas like kitchens and baths.

Do you help with colors and sheens?

Yes. We provide palette guidance, sheen recommendations by room, and in-situ test patches so you can see the color in real light.

What coatings do you use?

We select premium primers and topcoats for the surface and wear level‚Äîcabinet-grade enamels for millwork, elastomerics for masonry, and low-odor options for occupied spaces.

How do you handle exteriors?

Wash and prep first, address substrate repairs, prime for adhesion, then apply weather-specified topcoats. We plan around weather windows and protect landscaping.
